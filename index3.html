Courses Offered

  • Introduction to Computer Science I

    Introduction to principles of computer science and to the C++ programming language.

  • Introduction to Computer Science II

    Continuation of Introduction to Computer Science I. Deeper understanding of C++ and simple algorithms is emphasized.

  • Data Structures

    Analysis of how to create and implement data structures such as stacks, linked lists, queues, trees, and graphs.

  • Computer Architecture

    Introduction to hardware and organization of computer systems. Topics include circuits, assembly language, and memory.

  • Analysis of Algorithms

    Introduction to various algorithms and how to analyze them in terms of time and space complexity.

  • Computer Networking

    Introduction to computer networks and internet protocols/

  • Computer Databases

    Introduction on how to create and use databases.
